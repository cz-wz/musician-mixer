Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of skills do I need?

Be ready to jam with others! Keep a beat, listen to others, have a few chords and riffs ready. We're a welcoming community that will guide each other with the skills we have!

Do I need to bring my own instruments?

We've got a few amps, drum kit, and a jankety old keyboard. Bring anything else!

Is there a fee to participate?

We just need to cover the rental cost of the studio and gear, which comes out to about $10 per person. Pay what you can!

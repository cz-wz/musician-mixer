Frequently Asked Questions

What genres of music are welcome?

All genres are welcome! Whether you're into rock, jazz, classical, electronic, or any other style, we encourage you to bring your unique sound to the mixer.

Do I need to bring my own instruments?

While we have some basic instruments available, we recommend bringing your own to ensure you're comfortable and familiar with your gear.

Is there a fee to participate?

No, the Music Mixer is free to attend! We want to make music collaboration accessible to everyone in the community.
